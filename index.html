How To Play

Earn points by getting your player across to the water. On the way avoid the bugs and rocks all while collecting gems and stars.

How many times can you make it across?

Can you beat your highest score?

Buttons

Move Player
Activates Star
Restart the Game

Select your Player

Score System

Crossing

For every successful Cross 10 points will be rewarded.
However, if you have crossed more than:

20 times - each cross rewards 20 points.

30 times - each cross rewards 30 points.

40 times - each cross rewards 40 points.

Gems

Collect as many Gems as possible to increase your Score on the way to the swimming pool.
Orange Gem: 100 points
Green Gem: 200 points
Blue Gem: 300 points

Streak

Keep your Streak of crossing to the swiming pool as high as possible to multiple your score.
Streak higher than:

10 double the points you earn.

20 triples the points you earn.

30 quadruples the points you earn.

Stay Alive

You will start with 5 Hearts. Collect more Hearts and the Stars along the way to live longer...
Once your Heart gets to 0 you cannot be bitten by a bug anymore. If so Game Over!!
Star gives you a temporary invincibility. For 5 seconds Bugs won't be able to bite you and you will be able to climb over the rocks. Use it wisely. Hint: There can be too many rocks on your way!!!
